About Nudgie

Nudgie is a simple reminder app designed to help you keep track of the important little things—birthdays, anniversaries, home maintenance tasks, and more. We believe productivity should be easy, friendly, and private.

No complex dashboards. No unnecessary features. Just the right nudge at the right time.

Do more. Be present. Save the planet.
